EUS

Complex interpretation

Endoscopic Ultrasound (EUS) requires special training and expertise to interpret ultrasound images.

EUS is the key to both accurate, timely diagnosis as well as new cutting edge therapeutics such as RFA and localized radiation. Yet it remains hard to interpert and heavily operator dependant.

A perfect candidate for human-AI collaboration

A real-time AI assistant for endoscopists

Delivering instant interpretation of the ultrasound video to improve diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes.

Improve biopsies intake

AI-guided identification of optimal biopsy sites improves sample quality and diagnostic yield.

Enable cutting edge therapeutics

Advanced visualization and real-time guidance for complex endoscopic procedures and interventions.

Expand access to crucial care

Enabling more endoscopists to perform accurate EUS, bringing specialized care to patients everywhere.
