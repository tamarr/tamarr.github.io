Better Clinician Collaboration.
Better Hospital Billing.


VeraMed is the new hospital information pipeline - from the ground up. It starts with a better collaboration tool for clinicians, allowing them to reduce administrative load and increase productivity, and ends with improved billing outcomes.




Learn more
There are hundreds of tasks performed in a hospital department every single day. Currently most of them are managed informally, verbally, in text and beepers. On the other side of this equation is $300B in denied claims most of them, studies show, are due to incomplete documentation and homan error.

VeraMed improves and simplifies the medical team task management around patient care by offloading it into an easy to use app, built with well understood best practices. That live, real time information, is then converted into a more reliable billing data, allowing the hospitals to recover more of their claims.
