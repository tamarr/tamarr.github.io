Burnout has always been highest in healthcare and especially so in hospitals. Since covid it has reached an alarming 63% of physicians in the States. The estimated annual financial loss there is $260M in burnout related turnover alone, and almost $5B when included with other consequences of burnout such as reduced quality of care and increase in medical error.

A significant contributor to burnout in hospitals is cumbersome processes that are a result of the complexity of the system. Yet we believe this is not intrinsic and can be improved. We streamline hospital processes for the clinicians so that they can focus on the professional part of their work, what they came here to focus on - caring for patients.

We are currently designing an application that focuses on improving cross-department consultation processes. By adding a layer of accessibility and transparency from the moment a request is made until it is fulfilled, we remove uncertainty and reduce the effort required from clinicians.
